# Privacy Policy for Mood Wellness and Recipe

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

Mood Wellness and Recipe ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection and Storage

### Local Data Storage
- **Mood Logs**: All mood tracking data, including timestamps, mood types, and notes, are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SharedPreferences).
- **Recipes**: All user-created recipes, including images, ingredients, and instructions, are stored locally on your device using SQLite database.
- **App Settings**: User preferences, including dark mode settings and notification preferences, are stored locally on your device.

### No Data Transmission
- **No Internet Required**: This app operates completely offline. No data is transmitted to external servers.
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not use cloud storage services. All data remains on your device.
- **No Analytics**: We do not collect analytics data or usage statistics.
- **No Third-Party Services**: We do not share your data with third-party services for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose.

## Permissions

### Notification Permissions
- **Purpose**: We request notification permissions to send you reminders to track your mood.
- **Usage**: Notifications are used solely for mood tracking reminders. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.
- **Data**: No personal data is transmitted through notifications.

### Image Picker Permission
- **Purpose**: We request access to your device's photo library to allow you to add images to your recipes.
- **Usage**: Images are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.
- **Data**: Images are only accessed when you explicitly choose to add them to a recipe.

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage mechanisms.
- We do not have access to your data as it never leaves your device.
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app from your device.

## Your Rights

- **Access**: You can access all your data directly within the app.
- **Deletion**: You can delete individual mood entries or recipes at any time. To delete all data, uninstall the app.
- **Export**: You can export your mood data and recipes as PDF reports, which are stored locally on your device.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
